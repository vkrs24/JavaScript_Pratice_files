1. Arrays are References
        arr1=[1,2,3];
        arr2=arr1;
        arr2.push(4);
        which results in below one
                        =>arr1=[1,2,3,4]
                        =>arr2=[1,2,3,4]
        
        to resolve this we use slice() method in array
        arr1=[1,2,3];
        arr2=arr1.slice();
        arr2.push(4);
        which results in below one
                        =>arr1=[1,2,3]
                        =>arr2=[1,2,3,4]
